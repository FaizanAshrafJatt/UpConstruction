Welcome To Galaxy Gloves

A Legacy of Dedication and Excellence

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Arslan, Galaxy Gloves commenced its journey in the gloves industry in 1999 . Guided by unwavering dedication and a commitment to excellence, Arslan has become a respected name in Pakistan's workwear and glove sector. Today, Galaxy Gloves proudly stands as one of Pakistan's foremost producers and exporters of workwear and gloves, owing its success to in-house production capabilities and an unyielding pursuit of excellence. With expertise in crafting workwear and gloves, we offer premium products,impact-resistant gloves , and industrial gloves tailored for various sectors. Our strategically located production facilities serve customers worldwide. We've earned the trust of our valued clients. Our dedicated team's endless effort has made Galaxy Gloves a continued success story.
